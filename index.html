📜 My Story

I grew up in a country where Game Development as a profession didn't exist when I was graduating a high school. My childhood dream was to make video games, but I had to chose a 'real' profession.

I chose to study Telecommunication Engineering at the University, because I wanted to understand how information technologies work on the physical level! Getting a BSc in Radio / Telecom helped me build a solid foundation in Math and Physics, which helped me a lot in studying Programming in the future.

My early career was in Technical / Engineering Sales roles in Health Tech and IT industry, which helped me save up enough money to move to Vancouver.

In Vancouver, I've realized that a career in Game Development is more than just a dream! It inspired me to learn Programming and I succesfully switched my career into Software Development here!
